A bit about us

A SPACE TO
BE YOURSELF.

Dear Eleanor is a community space dedicated to creating low-key events for Miami locals—from art shows and live music to workshops and more.

We believe everyone deserves access to creative experiences, connection, and a welcoming room. So we make one. No posturing. No velvet rope. Just people, ideas, and the kind of nights that leave you inspired.
